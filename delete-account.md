# Delete Your Wirdi Account

Wirdi gives you full control over your account and data. You can permanently delete your account and all associated data at any time, directly from within the app.

## How to delete your account

1. Open the Wirdi app (iOS or Android)
2. Sign in to the account you want to delete
3. Tap the **Profile** tab (bottom right)
4. Scroll down to the **Account** section
5. Tap **Delete Account**
6. Enter your password to confirm
7. Tap **Delete**

## What gets deleted

When you delete your account, the following is **permanently and immediately removed**:

- Your user profile (display name, email, account identifier)
- Your friend list
- Sent and received invitations (friend requests and activity invitations)
- Your Firebase Authentication credentials
- Your stored push notification token
- Your premium subscription state on our servers (subscription billing is handled by Apple/Google and must be cancelled separately through the App Store or Google Play Store)

## What is retained

Some data is retained because it was shared with other users or is part of shared activities:

- **Shared activities**: activities you joined with other users remain accessible to those other users. Your progress contribution to those shared activities stays attributed.
- **Anonymized usage analytics**: aggregate, non-identifying usage data (feature adoption rates, crash diagnostics, etc.) may remain in our analytics systems.

None of the retained data can be used to personally identify you after deletion.

## Account deletion is permanent

There is no "undo." Once you confirm deletion, the account cannot be recovered. If you want to use Wirdi again after deletion, you'll need to sign up with a new account.
